About me

I love automation. Whether I'm writing scripts at work or tinkering with my home server, I find it so satisfying to make the things you do everyday easier.

For programming, I'm currently using 3 main languages: Rust, Python, and Go. I enjoy Rust because of how performant it is (not to mention the best compiler warnings I've ever experienced), Python because of how easy it is to write, and Go because it's situated perfectly in the middle of that.

My home server has been a blessing and a curse — every time I finish tidying up a new container, something else will rear its head that I've got to dive into. Home assistant, Zigbee, MMWave, Adguard, Mealie... the list goes on.

I also love Neovim. I started using Vim in college and I honestly don't think I could ever switch to another editor — or at least, not without vim-motions. Macros and plugins make every facet of my life (that has to do with text) easier.

Outside of the house, I enjoy: Running, Tennis, Pickleball, Soccer, and Skiing.
